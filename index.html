Fuudy

Revive food, sharing, and lives beside you

FUUDY is Canada's first tech-based charitable project aiming to reduce edible food waste and world hunger, through developing a technology-integrated and sustainable perishable food redistribution system based on a network of smart, community-based sharing fridges.
